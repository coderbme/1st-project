I. Types of Patient monitor

1. Patient monitor

A patient monitor is also called a medical monitor, a physiological monitor or a display, which is an electronic medical device. This instrument can measure the patient's vital signs, display the data obtained therefrom, and transmit it on the monitoring network.

2.Vital signs monitor

An intuitive touch screen monitor can be used to improve workflow and training. It is suitable for most low-sensitivity medical environments and clinical workflows, and can measure pulse, blood oxygen saturation, non-invasive blood pressure, temperature, EtCo2, respiration, etc.

3. End-tidal carbon dioxide monitor

It is an instrument used to measure end-tidal carbon dioxide (EtCO2). At the end of exhalation, a non-invasive technique is used to measure the partial pressure or maximum concentration of carbon dioxide (CO2).When purchasing the patient monitors, it's better to learn about their respective functions in advance and choose qualified patient monitor suppliers.

Ⅱ.Functional classifications of patient monitors

According to the function classification, there are three types of bedside monitors, central monitors and discharged monitors. They are divided into intelligent and non-intelligent.

1. Bedside monitor

It is an instrument connected to the patient's bedside and can continuously monitor various physiological parameters or certain states of the patient. The bedside monitor can display data, alarm and record information, and it can also work as a whole with the central patient monitor.

2. Central monitor

The central monitor consists of the main monitor and several bedside monitors. The main monitor can control the work of each bedside monitor, and monitor the conditions of multiple subjects at the same time. Its characteristic is that it can complete the automatic recording of various abnormal physiological parameters and medical records.

3. Discharge monitor

The out-of-hospital monitor is generally a small electronic patient monitor machine that the patient can carry with him. The instrument can continuously monitor certain physiological parameters of patients inside and outside the hospital, which can be used as a reference for doctors in diagnosis.
